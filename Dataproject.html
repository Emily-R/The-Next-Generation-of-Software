Data Project

As part of my Computer Science course, I had to do a data project. The problem I addressed was if mint actually cools things down. Many commercials for mints say that thier mints have a "cooling effect", and I wanted to find out if that is true. So, I did an expirament with three types of mints: Lifesavers Wint O Green, Mentos, and Myntz! (vanilla). I dissolved each one in hot water and recorded the temperature of each glass every five minutes. I then placed the data into a chart and a graph, as seen below.
